YouTube video downloader with SponsorBlock utility through their API. Downloads video & audio track seperately, removes sponsored segments and encodes both tracks together. Can be used to download independent audio files or a batch of YouTube videos from a text file.

Standalone script can be found from /dist

Requirements:
ffmpeg
